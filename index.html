Biography

My name is Guanghui Min(pronunciation: /ɡwɑːŋ-hweɪ mɪn/), a first-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at University of Virginia. I am very fortunate to be advised by Professor Chen Chen.

My current interests focus on Graph data mining and theory, network analysis as well as their applications in epidemiology, financial markets and so on.

I completed my Master's degree in Applied Statistics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2020, supervised by Professor Kerby Shedden. Prior to that, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Wuhan University. I worked as a full-time Software Engineer at the Innovation Center of Yinhua Fund Management Co., Ltd, a leading public fund(more than 1 trillion CNY AUM in 2021) in mainland China from 2020 to 2024.

Miscellaneous
